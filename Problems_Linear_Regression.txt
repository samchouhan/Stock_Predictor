After seeing the results based on Linear Regression model it is seen that the more the dataset 
the more is the accuracy of model but it is misleading.

With one year of dataset the future prediction showed around 30% of uptrend in ADANI POWER stock
and with 
5 year of dataset it showed 42% of bullish trend in the same stock with accuracy of model increased to 
0.9 from 0.6 ...

These numbers look good on the graph but in reality the stock I chose is volatile and depends on the market
trends too ...

So the problem with Linear Regression model is that it shows results solely based on dataset trend making
the numbers look less realistic . 

Hence, not good for predicting future of stocks .
